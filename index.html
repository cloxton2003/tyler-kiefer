About KIEFER

We are a family owned and operated company. With experience in all of the electrical industries. Our goal is to strive to keep our clients happy and safe with all of the work that we perform. We are proud to offer services in all sectors of the industry including; residential, commercial, industrial, marine, home audio systems, affordable home automation and networking.

Audio / Automation

The systems that we offer can be completely customizable to the individual needs of the client.
